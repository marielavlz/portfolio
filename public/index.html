About Me

Born in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico, I grew up in Aguadilla before I came to the mainland in 2008 where I attended high school and eventually college at the University of Texas at Austin. Although majoring in Microbiology and Infectious Disease, I took an MIS course which first exposed me to basic web development, IT, and business models. And from there, I played with the idea of pursuing a career in development and technology until one day I decided it was time to take the leap. I love the decision I made to pursue web development and take pride in my diverse cultural and educational background.
