About me

Hello! My name is Mariela Velez.

But call me Mari. I was born in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico where I lived until 2008 when I moved to the mainland USA at 13 years old. In Texas, I attended Tomball High School and recently graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor of Science in Microbiology and Infectious Diseases.

Afterwards, I chose to acquire a certification in Full Stack Web Development from UT Austin, having gathered interest in web development throughout my years as an undergrad. I am now proud to say that I have always pursued all of my passions and strove to excel in them, being both a young microbiologist and a web developer.

During my free time, I enjoy the works of JRR Tolkien, JK Rowling, and George RR Martin, my favourite genre being fantasy. I also love classic stories such as The Picture of Dorian Grey and To Kill a Mockingbird. Lastly, you can find me any time of the day sitting down and enjoying a hot cup of tea and sugar biscuits.
